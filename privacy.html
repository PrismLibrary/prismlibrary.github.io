Prism Library Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20 February 2024

Prism Software, LLC ("Prism", "we", "us", or "our") respects the privacy of our users ("you" or "your"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our website, prismlibrary.com (the "Site"), and our OAuth client integrated with Google (the "Client").

Information We Collect

We collect the following information from you:

  • Basic User Information: When you create an account or authenticate using an external OAuth provider (Microsoft, Google, or GitHub) with Azure Active Directory B2C, we collect your name and email address.
  • Subscription Information: If you purchase a license, we may associate your account with one or more subscriptions in Stripe, but we do not store any payment information ourselves.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with access to the Site and Client.
  • To process your license purchases and manage your subscriptions.
  • To communicate with you about your account, licenses, and subscriptions. This includes sending you emails when you create a new license, are assigned or unassigned a license, or your license is expiring.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

We do not:

  • Share your information with any third parties, except as required by law or to fulfill our contractual obligations.
  • Track your activity on the Site or Client beyond what is necessary to provide you with the services you request.
  • Use your information for any purpose other than those listed above.

External OAuth Providers

When you authenticate using an external OAuth provider, you are granting that provider permission to share certain information about you with us. The specific information that is shared will vary depending on the provider, but typically includes your name and email address. We recommend that you review the privacy policies of the external OAuth providers you use to understand how they collect, use, and share your information.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no website or internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Your Choices

You can access, update, or delete your information by contacting us at support@prismlibrary.com. You can also choose to no longer receive emails from us by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send you.

Children's Privacy

The Site and Client are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@prismlibrary.com.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Site. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@prismlibrary.com.
